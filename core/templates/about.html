BUILT FROM THE INSIDE OUT

At FastCredentials.com, we didn’t just study the healthcare industry—we live in it.

We are the providers, the support staff, and the operational teams. We are the ones working behind the scenes, ensuring patient care happens safely. We built this because we are you.

01. THE SPARK

Born in the Breakroom.

The idea for FastCredentials wasn't born in a boardroom; it was born out of frustration. Regardless of our roles, we all shared a common burden: The cost of going to work.

From expensive CMEs to Vendor Access Portals—and yes, even paying for the Scrub Vending Machines—it felt like we were constantly paying out of pocket just to stay compliant. We looked at the industry and saw a landscape that was complicated, expensive, and indifferent. We knew there had to be a better way.

02. THE MISSION

Healthcare Friendly. Wallet Friendly.

We founded FastCredentials.com on a simple, rebellious premise: Compliance shouldn't be a punishment.

We set out to create a one-stop, customer-focused destination that strips away the bureaucracy and the exorbitant fees. Our goal is to make sure that when you need a credential, you can get it quickly, easily, and at a much better value than the "big guys" charge.

03. QUALITY

Trust Built on Experience.

Being affordable doesn't mean cutting corners. Because we come from the industry, we take safety and education seriously.

Our state-of-the-art learning modules are rigorously constructed to meet or exceed industry standards. We built the exams and course materials we wished we had when we were in your shoes—clear, relevant, and efficient.

04. WHY US?

We Want You to Win.

When you choose FastCredentials, you aren't just buying a certificate; you are supporting a team that is rooting for you.

We are here to prove that you can provide the lowest price anywhere without sacrificing quality or customer care. We want to win because when we win, the hardworking people of the healthcare system win, too.
