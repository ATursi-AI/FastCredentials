01

Born in the Breakroom.

The idea for FastCredentials wasn't born in a boardroom. It was born out of frustration — the kind that builds quietly over years of watching good people get nickel-and-dimed just to stay employed.

Expensive CMEs. Vendor access portals. Annual renewals that cost more than a car payment. We kept asking the same question: why does it cost this much to go to work?
