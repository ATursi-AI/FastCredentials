Frequently Asked Questions

I completed a course, but where is my certificate?

Certificates are generated instantly upon passing the exam. You can find them in your Dashboard. Look for the "ACCESS CREDENTIAL" button on the specific course card.

Are these credentials accepted by major vendor systems?

Yes. Our modules are built to meet or exceed OSHA, CDC, and AdvaMed standards for 2026. Each certificate includes a unique verification ID and QR code that hospital administrators and vendor management systems can instantly verify.

I need a receipt for reimbursement.

We know the drill. A downloadable, itemized receipt is automatically emailed to you upon purchase. You can also download past receipts directly from your account settings page.

What if I fail an exam?

Don't sweat it. We are education-focused, not punitive. You can review the course material and retake the exam immediately at no extra cost. We want you to master the material, not pay extra fees.

How do I reset my password?

If you're locked out, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen. If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or email us at support@fastcredentials.com.
